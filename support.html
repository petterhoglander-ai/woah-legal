Support

Help, answers, and how to get in touch.

Woah is a recovery-tracking app that reads your sleep, heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, and other signals from Apple Health to give you a daily recovery score and one clear daily message. If you need help, the fastest way to reach us is by email — we read and reply to every message.

Contact us: petter@woah-recovery.app
We typically reply within 1–2 business days.

My recovery score isn't showing

Woah needs Apple Health data — ideally sleep and heart-rate variability from a wearable (Apple Watch, Garmin, Oura, Whoop, etc.). If your device has no data yet, the main screen shows a waiting state. You can tap "Enter data manually" on the main screen to enter values and see the full experience. Make sure Woah has permission in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Woah.

How do I connect a partner?

Open the partner tab and either share your 6-character code or enter your partner's code. Both people must agree to pair. You can disconnect at any time from within the app.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

The partner feature is an auto-renewable subscription. You can manage or cancel it, and turn off auto-renewal, in your Apple ID Account Settings (Settings → your name → Subscriptions). Solo use of Woah is free.

How do I delete my data?

Open the ••• menu → Profile → scroll down → Delete my data. This permanently removes your profile, history, partner connections, and all synced data from your device and iCloud.

Is Woah a medical device?

No. Woah is a general wellness and recovery-tracking tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.
